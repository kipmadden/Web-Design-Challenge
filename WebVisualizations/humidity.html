Latitude vs. Humidity %

Analysis & Observations: The Latitude vs. Humidity plot does not seem to have a strong correlation to latitude. One can say however, that it appears more cities have a humidity above 60% than a humidity below 60%. It will be interesting to see if grouping by Northern vs. Southern Hemispheres will tease this cluster apart to reveal a trend.
